What do We do?

MedicPlanet is a future innovation for the contact between the best sepcialists in the town and patients. Our objective is to shorten the process of finding a perfect specialist for our patient's needs.

MedicPlanet is located in Poland where we focus on improving the contact between the clinics hospitals and patients from various cities.

Our company is based on knowledge of our doctors who aim to solve your daily struggles. Don't hesitate and get in contact with us - it will only get better!

Our online consultations aim to solve the issue of distance and possible lack of choice in the smaller cities. Our doctors use the newest methods to make the conversation as efficient as possible.

You can also visit us in one of our locations and meet with the doctors and specialists. We are located in almost 20 cities and we are waiting for you!.

If you are not sure whether you need an online consultation or an appointment, we have created a MedicPlanet Assistant who aims to answer your basic questions and guides you through the process of choosing our service based on your needs.
